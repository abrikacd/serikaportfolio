I’m Rika, an independent artist who enjoys watching classic horror movies like The Birds and The Shining.
I started off sketching My Little Pony characters onto my math workbooks, creating imaginary plots
where Twilight Sparkle invited me for brunch and Queen Chrysalis had matching BFF bracelets with me.
Now, I draw to invoke new thoughts and discussions,
even if they come a few days late.

When I’m not testing out new, unconventional art-styles,
there’s a very high chance you can catch me painting my
dog, hushing her to not move as I finish the laaaaast brush stroke.
